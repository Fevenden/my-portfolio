About Me

I am a Web developer and currently attending Thinkful’s full-stack flex program. Coding is a natural passion for me. I grew up around computers, and after building my first one at 15, I wanted to learn everything I could about them. Creating web apps using abstract languages that only exist in a virtual world is like magic to me, and I will always be excited to use and practice it. I enjoy challenging myself, and many of my hobbies reflect this. You might find me at the gym lifting weights, playing video games, or hiking up a mountain. I have a passion for music, and you could say I know my way around a guitar. I am always looking for a new challenge, so whatever it is, bring it on.
